News Release

Perry Tech ticket raffle vital to provide scholarships for students

Perry Technical Institute in Yakima, Washington held its third annual "win a house" raffle with a chance to win a house by purchasing a $100 ticket. Although the raffle came up short of the number of tickets needed, the winner did get a 50/50 split of the total money raised of around $225,000 for student scholarships. School officials say these events are vital to help students who come from low-income families graduate from their programs.
